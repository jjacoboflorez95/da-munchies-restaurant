Margarita $5.99

A classic cocktail made with tequila, lime juice, and orange liqueur, typically served in a salt-rimmed glass. Can be served on the rocks or blended with ice for a frozen version. Variations may include fruit flavors such as strawberry or mango. Often paired with Mexican or Tex-Mex cuisine.

Old Fashioned $10.85

A traditional whiskey cocktail made with sugar, bitters, and a citrus peel, typically garnished with a cherry and orange slice. Can be made with bourbon or rye whiskey, and variations may include other types of spirits such as brandy or rum. Served over ice or straight up in a chilled glass. Often considered a classic or "vintage" drink option.
